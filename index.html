About Me

I am a technically adept Petroleum Engineering graduate with field experience in geothermal well-logging, drilling logistics, and reservoir management at KenGen's Olkaria site. My unique background combines traditional energy expertise with modern software development skills.

Proficient in data analysis for exploration using ArcGIS, satellite imagery, and Python, I have a solid foundation in SQL, Python, Git and SolidWorks. Pursuing a full-stack software development program with PLP, building database-driven web solutions for non-profits focused on climate resilience.

My key projects include wind energy site selection using ArcGIS and remote sensing data. I am passionate about integrating software engineering with energy systems, currently exploring applications of blockchain and machine learning in sustainable resource management.
